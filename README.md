## What is CompRoW@sh4ka?

**CompRoW@sh4ka** (Computational Row — Shaker) is a civic-tech project under the umbrella of ГО Digital web3cloud neuralinfra advanc hybrid inc — a Ukrainian civil society organization building secure digital infrastructure, Web3 governance, and AI-powered democratic tools.

The project combines **computational governance**, **blockchain transparency**, and **community-driven innovation** — shaking up traditional government–citizen interactions through cutting-edge technology.

## Core Pillars

### 🔐 Secure Governance Infrastructure
Quantum-safe encryption, zero-trust communications, and blockchain-verified audit trails for accountable public institutions.

### 🧠 AI-Powered Democratic Tools
Neural civic intelligence, predictive policy modeling, and simulation frameworks that transform reactive governance into proactive evidence-based decision-making.

### ⛓️ Web3 Transparency Layer
Trust mechanisms with blockchain-verified actions, public accountability scoring, and immutable audit trails — ensuring every government decision is traceable.

### 🌐 International Standards Compliance
Observer verification frameworks aligned with WIPO, IOM, OSPAR, and UNFCCC standards for global interoperability.
