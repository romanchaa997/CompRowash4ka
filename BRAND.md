# CompRoW@sh4ka — Brand Guide

## Brand Identity

### Name
- **Full**: CompRoW@sh4ka
- **Short/Display**: CompRoW
- **Tagline**: "Shaking up governance. Computationally."
- **Ukrainian**: "Обчислювальний зсув. Для демократії."

### Name Etymology
- **Comp** = Computational (обчислювальний)
- **Row** = Row of data / row of change / row of collaboration
- **@sh4ka** = Shaker (той, хто трусить / змінює систему) + .sh hacker aesthetics + leetspeak 4=a

### Positioning
CompRoW@sh4ka is not a corporate product. It's a civic-tech movement born from Ukrainian resilience — combining quantum computing, blockchain, and AI to build transparent, accountable governance.

**Tone**: Technical yet accessible. Patriotic but globally minded. Grant-oriented, not VC-pitch.

## Voice & Tone

### Writing Guidelines
1. **Bilingual by default** — Mix Ukrainian and English naturally, like the team speaks
2. **Technical substance** — Use real terms (quantum cryptography, zero-trust, blockchain audit trail), not buzzwords
3. **Grant-oriented** — Frame benefits in terms of social impact, democratic transparency, international standards compliance
4. **Ukraine-first** — Proudly Ukrainian innovation, not a Silicon Valley clone
5. **No hype** — Backed by data (12,247 pilot users, 87.3% completion) not promises

### Vocabulary
| Use ✅ | Avoid ❌ |
|--------|---------|
| Civic infrastructure | Disruptive technology |
| Democratic innovation | Revolutionary product |
| Transparent governance | World-changing solution |
| Evidence-based policy | AI-powered everything |
| Community-driven | User-centric |
| Standards-compliant | Best-in-class |
